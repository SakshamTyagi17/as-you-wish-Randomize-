AI Powered Meeting Summary Generator

Effortlessly capture, summarize, and analyze your meeting discussions with advanced AI tools.

Features

Speech-to-Text Conversion: Converts spoken words to text in real-time for accurate transcription.
Real-Time Summarization: Provides instant summaries during meetings for quick understanding.
Keyword Extraction: Extracts important keywords for easy reference and retrieval.
Bullet Point Summaries: Generates concise bullet point summaries for clarity and readability.
Action Items Tracker: Automatically tracks decisions made and tasks assigned during meetings.
Sentiment Analysis: Analyzes the tone of conversations to provide better insights.

Process

Step 1: Input Audio/Text- Upload audio files or input text directly for analysis.
Step 2: Analysis- The AI breaks down the data to identify essential points.
Step 3: Text Conversion- Converts speech to text accurately.
Step 4: Summarization- Generates concise summaries from detailed inputs.
Step 5: Keyword Extraction- Highlights crucial keywords for better comprehension.
Step 6: Analytics & Export- Provides insights and allows exporting results.
